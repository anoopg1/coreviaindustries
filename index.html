Our Story

Built for the
Long Roll.

Corevia Industries was founded with a clear vision — to become India's most reliable thermal paper manufacturer and distributor. Starting from our precision slitting unit in Alappuzha, Kerala, we process bulk jumbo rolls into perfectly finished thermal paper rolls for every commercial application.

We are a manufacturer-first company. Every roll that leaves our facility goes through strict quality checks — correct width tolerance, consistent density, clean edges. We supply direct to businesses, cutting out the middleman and passing the value to our customers.

Our 10-year vision: to be the number one thermal paper importer and distributor in India — and we are building every process, every partnership, and every product with that north star in mind.
